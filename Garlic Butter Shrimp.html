Garlic Butter Shrimp

Juicy shrimp tossed in rich garlic butter with a fresh squeeze of lemon — simple, elegant, and ready in minutes.
This dish is simple, elegant, and absolutely irresistible✨

🛒Ingredients

  • 500g large shrimp (peeled & deveined)
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt & black pepper (to taste)
  • ½ teaspoon chili flakes (optional, for a little heat)
  • Juice of ½–1 fresh lemon
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley (chopped)

👩‍🍳Steps

  1. Pat the shrimp dry with paper towels. This helps them sear beautifully instead of steaming.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the olive oil and butter.
  3. Once the butter is melted, add the minced garlic and sauté for about 30 seconds until fragrant (do not let it brown).
  4. Add the shrimp in a single layer. Cook for 2–3 minutes until pink on one side.
  5. Flip the shrimp, season with salt, pepper, and chili flakes. Cook for another 2 minutes until fully opaque.
  6. Finish with fresh lemon juice and sprinkle chopped parsley on top. Toss gently and remove from heat.

✨ Pro Tips

- Don’t overcook the shrimp — they cook fast and can turn rubbery.
- Use fresh lemon juice for the brightest flavor.
- Add a splash of white cooking cream for a creamy garlic butter variation.

🍽 Serving Suggestions

  • Serve over buttery pasta or steamed rice.
  • Pair with toasted bread to soak up the sauce.
  • Add it to a fresh green salad for a light, protein-packed meal.
