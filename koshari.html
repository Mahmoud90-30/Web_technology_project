Koshari

Koshari is Egypt’s ultimate comfort food
a delicious mix of rice, lentils, pasta, chickpeas, crispy onions, and flavorful spicy tomato sauce.
Simple ingredients come together to create something absolutely amazing

📝Ingredients

  • Rice & Lentils

    • 1 cup rice
    • 1 cup brown lentils

  • Pasta

    • 1 cup small pasta (elbow pasta or broken spaghetti)

  • Chickpeas

    • 1 cup cooked chickpeas

  • Crispy Onions

    • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
    • Oil for frying

  • Tomato Sauce

    • 2 cups tomato puree
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tablespoon vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon cumin
    • 1 teaspoon coriander
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    • 1 tablespoon oil

👩‍🍳Steps

  1. Cook the Lentils

    • Rinse lentils well.
    • Boil in 3 cups of water for 20–25 minutes until tender but not mushy
    • Drain and set aside.

  2. Cook the Rice

    • Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pot.
    • Add rice and toast lightly.
    • Add 2 cups water and salt.
    • Cook on low heat until fully done.

    ✨ Tip: Some people mix lentils with rice while cooking — both methods are correct!

  3. Cook the Pasta

    • Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
    • Drain and set aside

  4. Prepare the Crispy Onions

    • Heat oil in a pan.
    • Fry sliced onions until deep golden brown
    • Place on paper towels to remove excess oil.

    Save some of the onion oil for the sauce — it adds extra flavor!

  5. Make the Tomato Sauce

    • Heat 1 tablespoon of onion oil in a pan
    • Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant
    • Add tomato puree, salt, pepper, cumin, and coriander

    Simmer for 10–15 minutes.

🍽 How to Assemble Koshari

  • Layer the ingredients in this order :

  • Rice
  • Lentils
  • Pasta
  • Chickpeas
  • Tomato sauce
  • Finish with lots of crispy onions on top ✨

🔥 Pro Tips
The secret of great koshari is in the crispy onions and the sauce.
Let the sauce have a slightly tangy taste.
Serve warm with extra sauce on the side.
