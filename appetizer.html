Caesar Salad 🥗

Ingredients

  • 1 lettuce head
  • 50 g parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup croutons
  • 2 tbsp Caesar dressing
  • 1 grilled chicken breast (optional)

Steps

  1. Wash and chop the lettuce.
  2. Add croutons and parmesan cheese.
  3. Add grilled chicken if desired.
  4. Pour dressing and mix well.

Greek Salad 🥗

Ingredients

  • 2 tomatoes
  • 1 cucumber
  • 50 g feta cheese
  • 1/4 onion
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and oregano

Steps

  1. Chop tomatoes and cucumber.
  2. Add sliced onion.
  3. Add feta cheese cubes.
  4. Add olive oil and oregano.
  5. Mix gently and serve.

Cheese Sambosa 🥟

Ingredients

  • 10 sambosa sheets
  • 150 g mozzarella cheese
  • 1 tbsp parsley
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix cheese with parsley.
  2. Fill sambosa sheets with mixture.
  3. Fold into triangle shape.
  4. Fry in hot oil until golden.

Kobeba 🥟

Ingredients

  • 10 kobeba shells (or dough sheets)
  • 200 g minced meat
  • 1 small onion
  • 1 tsp allspice
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Cook minced meat with onion and allspice.
  2. Add salt and pepper.
  3. Fill kobeba shells with meat mixture.
  4. Fold and fry until golden brown and crispy.

Garlic Bread 🫓

Ingredients

  • 1 baguette bread
  • 50 g butter
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tbsp parsley

Steps

  1. Mix butter with garlic and parsley.
  2. Spread mixture on bread.
  3. Bake for 10 minutes.
  4. Serve warm.

Cheese Sticks 🧀

Ingredients

  • 200 g mozzarella cheese sticks
  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 egg
  • 1 cup breadcrumbs
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Cut mozzarella into stick shapes.
  2. Dip each stick in flour.
  3. Dip in beaten egg.
  4. Coat with breadcrumbs.
  5. Fry in hot oil until golden.

Spring Rolls 🥟

Ingredients

  • 10 spring roll sheets
  • 1 cup shredded cabbage
  • 1 grated carrot
  • 1/2 cup cooked chicken (optional)
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Oil for frying

Steps

  1. Mix cabbage, carrot, and chicken.
  2. Add soy sauce.
  3. Place mixture inside spring roll sheet.
  4. Roll tightly and seal edges.
  5. Fry until crispy and golden.
