Chocolate Cake 🍫

For 5 Servings (persons)

📝Ingredients

Dry Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour (125 g)
  • 1/2 cup unsweetened cocoa powder (50 g) 🍫
  • 1 cup sugar (200 g) 🍚
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt 🧂

Wet Ingredients

  • 1 large egg 🥚 (room temperature)
  • 1/2 cup warm milk (120 ml) 🥛
  • 1/4 cup oil (60 ml) 🛢️
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla 🌼
  • 1/2 cup boiling water (120 ml) 💧

👩‍🍳Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare the Oven and Pan:
    • Preheat your oven to 180°C (350°F).
    • Grease an 18–20 cm round pan with butter or oil.
    • Lightly dust with flour OR line with parchment paper to prevent sticking.

  2. Mix the Dry Ingredients:
    • Sift together the flour and cocoa powder.
    • Add sugar, baking powder, baking soda, and salt.
    • Mix well using a whisk.
    • Sifting removes lumps and makes the cake softer.

  3. Add the Wet Ingredients:
    • To the same bowl, add egg, warm milk, oil, and vanilla.
    • Whisk for 1–2 minutes until the batter becomes smooth and slightly thick.

  4. Add the Boiling Water (Important Step):
    • Slowly pour in the boiling water while mixing gently.
    • The batter will become thin and liquid — this is normal.
    • The hot water helps bloom the cocoa and makes the cake extra moist.

  5. Bake:
    • Pour the batter into the prepared pan.
    • Place in the middle rack of the oven.
    • Bake for 25–30 minutes.
    • To test: Insert a toothpick in the center. If it comes out clean or with a few crumbs, it’s ready.

  6. Cool Down:
    • Let the cake cool in the pan for 10 minutes.
    • Remove from the pan and let it cool completely before frosting.

✨Optional Simple Chocolate Ganache (For Topping)

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup heavy cream (120 ml) 🥛
  • 100 g chocolate (chopped) 🍫

Steps

  • Heat the cream until it just starts to simmer (do not boil hard).
  • Pour it over the chopped chocolate.
  • Let sit for 1 minute. Stir until smooth.
  • Let it cool, then pour over the cake.

Enjoy your delicious chocolate cake! 🍫😍

Chocolate Chip Cookies 🍪

📝Ingredients

For 30 cookies

  • 2 cups butter (460 g), melted 🧈
  • 2 cups brown sugar (440 g), packed 🍯
  • 2 cups granulated sugar (400 g) 🍚
  • 4 large eggs 🥚
  • 1 tablespoon vanilla extract
  • 5 cups all-purpose flour (625 g)
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 4 cups chocolate chips (700 g) 🍫

👩‍🍳Preparation

  1. Prepare the Oven:
    • Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C)
    • Line a baking tray with parchment paper

  2. Mix Sugars & Butter:
    • Whisk melted butter with brown and granulated sugar until light and smooth

  3. Add Eggs & Vanilla:
    • Mix in eggs and vanilla extract until fully combined

  4. Add Dry Ingredients:
    • Add flour and baking powder, fold gently until soft dough forms

  5. Add Chocolate Chips:
    • Fold in chocolate chips evenly

  6. Shape the Cookies:
    • Form equal-sized balls of dough on the tray

  7. Bake:
    • Bake for 12 minutes, let cool slightly, then serve

Your cookies are ready! 🍪
Grab one 😍 and enjoy the sweet deliciousness 🤩

Donuts🍩

📝Ingredients

  • 3 cups all-purpose flour (plus extra for kneading)
  • 6 teaspoons sugar
  • 1 teaspoons salt
  • 3 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 cup warm milk🥛
  • 2 eggs 🥚
  • 60 g unsalted butter 🧈, softened
  • Oil for frying 🛢️

Optional Sauce (serve separately)

  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 30–45 ml milk 🥛 (adjust for desired consistency)
  • 1 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • Optional: cocoa 🍫, chocolate sauce 🍫, strawberry 🍓, or any flavor you like

👩‍🍳Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • In a large bowl, combine flour, sugar, and salt
    • Dissolve yeast in warm milk and let sit 5 minutes until frothy
    • Add the yeast mixture and eggs gradually, then start kneading
    • Add softened butter gradually and knead until smooth and elastic

  2. Let the Dough Rise:
    • Cover and place in a warm spot for 1 hour or until doubled in size

  3. Shape the Donuts:
    • Roll out the dough to 1–2 cm thickness
    • Cut out circles using a donut cutter or a cup, and make a smaller hole in the center

  4. Fry the Donuts:
    • Heat oil in a deep pan over medium heat
    • Fry each donut 2 minutes per side until golden brown
    • Drain on paper towels to remove excess oil

  5. Prepare the Sauce:
    • Mix powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla until smooth and creamy.
    • Optional: Add cocoa, chocolate, or fruit flavor as desired.
    • Serve in a separate bowl for everyone to dip or drizzle as they like.

  6. Serve:
    • Place donuts on a serving plate.
    • Let everyone add the sauce according to their taste.

Dig in and enjoy your Donuts🍩😍

Strawberry Ice Cream 🍨

📝Ingredients

  • 2 cups fresh strawberries 🍓, washed and chopped
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk 🥛
  • 3/4 cup sugar 🧂
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Extra strawberry pieces for topping 🍓

👩‍🍳Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare the Strawberry Puree:
    • Place 2 cups of chopped strawberries and 1/4 cup sugar in a blender.
    • Blend until smooth to make a strawberry puree.

  2. Mix Ice Cream Base:
    • In a large bowl, whisk together heavy cream, milk, remaining sugar, and vanilla extract.
    • Add the strawberry puree and mix well until fully combined.

  3. Freeze the Ice Cream:
    • Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn
      according to the manufacturer's instructions until it thickens.
    • If you don’t have an ice cream maker, pour into a freezer-safe container,
      and cover for 2–3 hours until smooth and creamy.

  4. Add Fresh Strawberry Pieces:
    • Fold in some chopped strawberries into the ice cream before the final freeze.

  5. Serving:
    • Scoop the ice cream into bowls or cones.
    • Top with extra fresh strawberry pieces 🍓 for a fresh, fruity taste.

Enjoy your strawberry ice cream 🍨
Creamy, sweet, and full of fresh strawberry flavor 😍🍓

Tiramisu

📝Ingredients

  • 1 cup black coffee ☕ (cooled)
  • 1 pack Lady Fingers (or any finger biscuits)
  • 250 ml ready-to-whip whipped cream 🥛
  • 100 g cream cheese (like Kiri or Philadelphia)
  • 2-3 tablespoons powdered sugar (adjust to taste)
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Cocoa powder for dusting 🍫

👩‍🍳Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare the Coffee:
    • Brew your coffee and let it cool completely.

  2. Make the Cream:
    • In a bowl, combine whipped cream, cream cheese, powdered sugar, and vanilla.
    • Whip until smooth and creamy.

  3. Prepare the Biscuits:
    • Quickly dip each Lady Finger into the coffee (don’t soak them too long or they’ll get soggy).
    • Arrange a layer of soaked biscuits in your serving dish.

  4. Add the Cream:
    • Spread half of the cream mixture over the biscuit layer.
    • Add a second layer of coffee-soaked biscuits.
    • Spread the remaining cream on top and smooth the surface.

  5. Chill:
    • Refrigerate for at least 2 hours (overnight is best).

  6. Serve:
    • Before serving, dust the top with cocoa powder.
    • Optionally, sprinkle some grated chocolate or chocolate chips for extra flavor.

Enjoy your Tiramisu😍

Cinnamon Rolls

📝 Ingredients

For the Dough:

  • 240 ml warm milk
  • 7 g instant yeast
  • 1 egg
  • 60 g melted butter
  • 3 g salt

For the Cinnamon Filling:

  • 115 g soft butter
  • 150 g brown sugar
  • 8 g cinnamon

For the Glaze:

  • 120 g powdered sugar
  • 30–45 ml milk
  • 2 ml vanilla

👩‍🍳Preparation Steps

  1. Make the Dough:
    • Mix warm milk, sugar, and yeast. Let sit 5–10 minutes.
    • Add egg, melted butter, and salt.
    • Add flour gradually and knead 8–10 minutes.
    • Cover and let rise for 1 hour.

  2. Prepare the Filling:
    • Mix butter, brown sugar, and cinnamon.

  3. Shape the Rolls:
    • Roll dough into a rectangle.
    • Spread filling evenly.
    • Roll tightly and cut into 9–12 pieces.
    • Place in greased pan and let rise 30–45 minutes.

  4. Bake:
    • Bake for 18–22 minutes.

  5. Add Glaze:
    • Mix glaze ingredients and pour over warm rolls.

✨ Pro Tip

Pour 2–3 tablespoons of warm cream over the rolls before baking for extra softness!

Enjoy your Cinnamon Rolls😍

Basbousa

📝Ingredients

  • 2 cups fine semolina
  • 1 cup sugar (200 g)
  • 1 cup yogurt (240 ml)
  • 1/2 cup melted butter or oil (120 ml)
  • 1/2 cup milk (120 ml)
  • 1 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla
  • Almonds for topping

👩‍🍳Preparation Steps

  1. Sugar Syrup (Sharbat):
    • In a saucepan, add sugar and water.
    • Bring to boil and cook for 5–7 minutes.
    • Add lemon juice and vanilla.
    • Turn off heat and let it cool completely.

  2. Prepare the Batter:
    • In a large bowl, mix semolina and sugar.
    • Add yogurt, melted butter, milk, vanilla, and baking powder.
    • Mix until well combined.
    • Let the mixture rest for 10 minutes.

  3. Prepare the Pan:
    • Grease a baking pan with butter.
    • Pour the batter and spread evenly.
    • Cut into squares or diamonds.
    • Place an almond on each piece.

  4. Bake:
    • Preheat oven to 180°C.
    • Bake for 30–35 minutes until golden brown.

  5. Add the Syrup:
    • Pour the cold syrup over the hot basbousa immediately after baking.
    • Let it absorb completely before serving.

Enjoy your Basbousa!😍
