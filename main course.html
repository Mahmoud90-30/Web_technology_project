Warak 3nab with Pomegranate 🍃

📝Ingredients

  • 500g grape leaves 🍃 (fresh or jarred, washed and drained)
  • 1 cup Egyptian rice 🍚 (washed and soaked 15 minutes)
  • 1 small onion 🧅, finely chopped
  • ½ cup chopped parsley 🌿
  • ¼ cup chopped dill 🌱
  • ½ cup chopped cilantro 🌿
  • 2 tablespoons tomato paste 🍅
  • Salt and black pepper 🧂 to taste
  • Juice of 1 lemon 🍋
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil 🫒 or vegetable oil

✨Garnish

  • Fresh pomegranate seeds (about ½ cup)
  • Extra lemon juice 🍋 (optional)

👩‍🍳Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare the Filling:
    • In a large bowl, mix the washed rice with onion, parsley, dill, and cilantro.
    • Add tomato paste, salt, pepper, and a pinch of spices.
    • Stir well until all ingredients are evenly combined.

  2. Prepare the Grape Leaves:
    • If using jarred leaves, rinse them well and soak in hot water for 5 minutes to soften.
    • Lay a leaf flat on a clean surface, and trim any tough stems.

  3. Stuff the Grape Leaves:
    • Place a small amount of filling (about 1 teaspoon) on each leaf.
    • Roll the leaf into a small cylinder or tube, folding the sides inward to secure the filling.

  4. Arrange in the Pot:
    • Place some grape leaves or potato slices at the bottom of the pot to prevent sticking.
    • Arrange the stuffed leaves tightly in a circular or stacked pattern.

  5. Cooking:
    • Mix hot water with lemon juice and olive oil.
    • Pour the mixture over the stuffed leaves until they are covered.
    • Place a heavy plate on top to keep them in place while cooking.
    • Cover the pot and cook over low heat for about 45 minutes to 1 hour, until fully cooked.

  6. Serving:
    • Transfer the stuffed leaves to a serving plate.
    • Sprinkle fresh pomegranate seeds on top.
    • ✨Optionally, drizzle extra lemon juice for a tangy flavor.

Enjoy the flavors you just created 💚

Molokhia (Egyptian Style)

📝Ingredients

  • 500g molokhia leaves 🥬 (fresh minced or frozen)
  • 4 cups chicken or beef broth (soup)🍲
  • 4 garlic cloves 🧄 (minced)
  • 1 tablespoon dried coriander 🌿
  • 1 tablespoon ghee or butter 🧈
  • Salt and black pepper 🧂 to taste

🍗For the Broth

  • 1 whole chicken 🍗 (or ½ kg beef)
  • 1 onion 🧅 (cut in half)
  • 1 bay leaf 🍃
  • Salt and black pepper 🧂

👩‍🍳Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare the Broth:
    • Place the chicken (or beef) in a large pot and cover with water.
    • Add onion,and bay leaf.
    • Bring to a boil and remove any foam from the surface.
    • Add salt and pepper, reduce heat, and cook for 40–45 minutes.
    • Remove the chicken and strain the broth.

  2. Prepare the Molokhia:
    • Heat 4 cups of broth in a pot (do not let it boil strongly).
    • Add molokhia gradually while stirring continuously.
    • Keep on low heat and avoid boiling to prevent separation.

  3. Prepare the Garlic Topping (Tasha):
    • Heat ghee or butter in a small pan.
    • Add minced garlic and sauté until golden.
    • Add dried coriander and stir for a few seconds.
    • Pour immediately over the molokhia and stir once.

  4. Serving:
    • pour the hot Molokhia into a serving dish.
    • ✨optionally serve with Rice OR Bread shaping like (cat ears) 🐱

Enjoy your homemade Molokhia!
Warm, comforting, and full of flavor 💚

Koshari

📝 Ingredients

  • Rice & Lentils

    • 1 cup rice
    • 1 cup brown lentils

  • Pasta

    • 1 cup small pasta (elbow pasta or broken spaghetti)

  • Chickpeas

    • 1 cup cooked chickpeas

  • Crispy Onions

    • 2 large onions, thinly sliced
    • Oil for frying

  • Tomato Sauce

    • 2 cups tomato puree
    • 3 cloves garlic, minced
    • 1 tablespoon vinegar
    • 1 teaspoon cumin
    • 1 teaspoon coriander
    • Salt and black pepper to taste
    • 1 tablespoon oil

👩‍🍳 Steps

  1. Cook the Lentils

    • Rinse lentils well.
    • Boil in 3 cups of water for 20–25 minutes until tender but not mushy.
    • Drain and set aside.

  2. Cook the Rice

    • Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pot.
    • Add rice and toast lightly.
    • Add 2 cups water and salt.
    • Cook on low heat until fully done.

    ✨ Tip: Some people mix lentils with rice while cooking — both methods are correct!

  3. Cook the Pasta

    • Boil pasta in salted water until al dente.
    • Drain and set aside.

  4. Prepare the Crispy Onions

    • Heat oil in a pan.
    • Fry sliced onions until deep golden brown.
    • Place on paper towels to remove excess oil.

    Save some of the onion oil for the sauce — it adds extra flavor!

  5. Make the Tomato Sauce

    • Heat 1 tablespoon of onion oil in a pan.
    • Add minced garlic and sauté until fragrant.
    • Add tomato puree, salt, pepper, cumin, and coriander.
    • Simmer for 10–15 minutes.

🍽 How to Assemble Koshari

  • Layer the ingredients in this order:

  • Rice
  • Lentils
  • Pasta
  • Chickpeas
  • Tomato sauce
  • Finish with lots of crispy onions on top ✨

🔥 Pro Tips
The secret of great koshari is in the crispy onions and the sauce.
Let the sauce have a slightly tangy taste.
Serve warm with extra sauce on the side.

Macaroni Béchamel

📝 Ingredients

  • For the pasta:

    • 500g penne or macaroni

  • For the meat sauce:

    • 500g minced beef
    • 1 chopped onion
    • 2 minced garlic cloves
    • 2 cups tomato sauce
    • 1 tbsp tomato paste
    • Salt and black pepper

  • For the béchamel sauce:

    • 4 tbsp butter
    • 4 tbsp flour
    • 4 cups warm milk
    • Salt and white pepper

👩🏻‍🍳 Steps

  1. Cook the pasta

    • Boil pasta in salted water until about 80% done.
    • Drain and set aside.

  2. Prepare the meat sauce

    • Heat oil in a pan.
    • Add onion and sauté until soft.
    • Add garlic, then minced meat and cook until browned.
    • Add tomato sauce, tomato paste, salt, pepper, and cinnamon.
    • Simmer for 10–15 minutes until the sauce thickens.

  3. Make the béchamel sauce

    • Melt butter in a pot.
    • Add flour and whisk for 1–2 minutes (avoid browning).
    • Gradually pour warm milk while whisking continuously.
    • Keep stirring until the sauce becomes thick and smooth.
    • Add salt, white pepper, and nutmeg.

    👉 The perfect béchamel texture should be creamy — not too thick or too runny.

  4. Assemble the dish

    • Mix half of the béchamel with the pasta.
    • In a baking dish, layer:
    • Half of the pasta
    • All the meat sauce
    • The remaining pasta
    • Pour the rest of the béchamel on top

    ✨ Optional: Add shredded mozzarella cheese on top.

  5. Bake

    • Bake at 180°C for 30–40 minutes until the top turns golden.
    • Let it rest for 10–15 minutes before cutting and serving.

Garlic Butter Shrimp

🛒 Ingredients

  • 500g large shrimp (peeled & deveined)
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 4 cloves garlic (minced)
  • Salt & black pepper (to taste)
  • ½ teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • Juice of ½–1 fresh lemon
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley (chopped)

👩‍🍳 Steps

  1. Pat the shrimp dry with paper towels.
  2. Heat skillet and melt butter with olive oil.
  3. Add garlic and sauté for 30 seconds.
  4. Add shrimp and cook 2–3 minutes per side.
  5. Season and finish with lemon juice and parsley.

✨ Pro Tips

- Don’t overcook the shrimp — they cook fast and can turn rubbery.
- Use fresh lemon juice for the brightest flavor.
- Add a splash of white cooking cream for a creamy variation.

🍽 Serving Suggestions

  • Serve over buttery pasta or steamed rice.
  • Pair with toasted bread.
  • Add to a fresh green salad.

Simple ingredients. Big flavor. Pure comfort on a plate. 🤍

Homemade Pizza🍕

📝Ingredients

  • 3 cups flour
  • 1 tablespoon instant yeast
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon salt 🧂
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 1 cup warm water 💧(as needed)

🍅For the Sauce

  • 1 cup tomato sauce 🍅
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic 🧄
  • 1 teaspoon oregano
  • Salt and black pepper 🧂
  • A small pinch of sugar

🧀Toppings (Optional)

  • Sausage
  • Pastrami
  • Chicken
  • Tuna
  • Olives 🫒
  • Bell peppers 🌶️
  • Mushrooms 🍄
  • Tomatoes 🍅
  • Mozzarella cheese 🧀

You can add any toppings you like based on your personal preference ❤️

👩‍🍳Preparation Steps

  1. Prepare the Dough:
    • Mix flour, yeast, sugar, and salt in a bowl.
    • Add oil and mix well.
    • Gradually add warm water and knead until soft and smooth.
    • Cover and let it rise for about 1 hour until doubled in size.

  2. Prepare the Sauce:
    • In a small pan, add tomato sauce.
    • Add garlic, salt, pepper, oregano, and sugar.
    • Simmer for 5 minutes on low heat, then turn off the heat.

  3. Assemble the Pizza:
    • Preheat oven to 200°C.
    • Roll out the dough in a greased baking pan.
    • Spread the sauce evenly.
    • Add mozzarella cheese.
    • Add your favorite toppings.
    • Bake for 15–20 minutes until golden and fully cooked.
  4. Serving:
    • Cut the pizza using a pizza cutter or a sharp knife.
    • Serve it hot on a large plate or wooden board.
    • ✨Optional: Add dipping sauce, extra cheese, or fresh basil for garnish.

Enjoy your homemade pizza🍕
Made with love and your favorite toppings 🍕😍
